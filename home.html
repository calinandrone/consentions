Weekly Wine Tasting

Join us every Thursday at 4pm for a weekly wine tasting in the cellar. Led by Sommelier Peter Benet, these popular events feature samles of his favorite wines from around the world. The event is free to attend, and cases of the featured wines are available afterwards and a 10% discount.

Award-Winning Food

In the tradition of Italy, our chefs make every attempt to use locally-sourced meats, cheeses, and produce whenever possible, and our pastas and pizza doughts are made fresh every day. This philosophy combined with our chefs' talents has garnered awards from Yelp, OpenTable, and more.

Top Chefs from Italy

Our team of chefs is led by Executive Chef Anthony D'Abruzzi. Anthony was born in Italy and came to Maryland with his family when he was young. He attended the Culinary Institute of America, and has worked in many of Washington, DC finest restaurants.
